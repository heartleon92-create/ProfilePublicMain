About the Designer

Su Hlaing is known for her innovative blend of traditional Myanmar textiles with contemporary silhouettes. Her designs have been featured in Paris Fashion Week and Vogue Magazine.

With a focus on sustainable fashion and ethical production, she creates pieces that tell a story while pushing the boundaries of modern fashion.
