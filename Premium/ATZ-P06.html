About Chef Kyaw

With over 15 years of culinary excellence, Chef Kyaw Zin Htet brings a unique fusion of traditional Burmese flavors with modern French techniques. His innovative approach to Southeast Asian cuisine has earned him international recognition.

Trained in Paris and Tokyo, Chef Kyaw believes in using only the freshest local ingredients to create memorable dining experiences that engage all the senses.
